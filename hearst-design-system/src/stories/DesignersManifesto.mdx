<Principle num="01" title="The Handoff is Dead">
  <Line>If your work ends in Figma, it's unfinished.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>Design does not get "implemented" by someone else.</Line>
  <Highlight>Design gets built. By you.</Highlight>
  <Whisper>The best work happens when the person who imagined it is the same person who sees it through.</Whisper>
</Principle>

<Principle num="02" title="Designers Ship">
  <Line>The barrier between design and production used to be a wall.</Line>
  <Line>AI turned it into a door.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>You don't wait for pull requests.</Line>
  <Line>You <Bold>create</Bold> them.</Line>
  <Line>You <Bold>test</Bold> them.</Line>
  <Line>You <Bold>own</Bold> them.</Line>
  <Whisper>You don't need to know React. You need to know what you want. Describe it in plain language. The AI handles the translation. You handle the vision.</Whisper>
</Principle>

<Principle num="03" title="The Browser is the Canvas">
  <Line>Not Figma. Not static mocks. Not a prototype.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>The real product lives in the browser —</Line>
  <Line>that's where real people experience your work.</Line>
  <Highlight>Design where it matters.</Highlight>
</Principle>

<Principle num="04" title="QA Starts With the Designer">
  <Line>Nobody on this team cares more about the experience than you do.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>The person who cares most</Line>
  <Line>should be the <Bold>first</Bold> to validate it.</Line>
  <Highlight>That's not QA's job first. That's yours.</Highlight>
  <Whisper>If something feels off at 320px on a Tuesday night, trust that instinct. Open the branch. Fix it. Ship it.</Whisper>
</Principle>

<Principle num="05" title="Iterate Where It's Real">
  <Line>Not four layers removed.</Line>
  <Line>Not in abstract components disconnected from context.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>In the <Bold>actual product</Bold>.</Line>
  <Line>In real states. Real data. Real edge cases.</Line>
  <Whisper>The gap between the design and the product is where quality dies. Close the gap.</Whisper>
</Principle>

<Principle num="06" title="Branches Are the New Playground">
  <Line>A Git branch is not scary. It's a sandbox.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>Explore freely. Break things safely. Refine endlessly.</Line>
  <Line>Nothing goes live until you say so.</Line>
  <Highlight>Every great idea deserves a branch to prove itself.</Highlight>
</Principle>

<Principle num="07" title="Engineers Are Amplifiers, Not Gatekeepers">
  <Line>Collaboration has never meant dependency.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>Design moves forward <Bold>with</Bold> engineers — not after them.</Line>
  <Line>You don't wait in a queue. You work in parallel.</Line>
  <Highlight>When designers ship, engineers build bigger.</Highlight>
  <Whisper>The best engineering teams want designers who push the product forward. Be the designer they want to work with.</Whisper>
</Principle>

<Principle num="08" title="Taste + Execution = Power">
  <Line>Taste alone is a mood board nobody opened.</Line>
  <Line>Execution alone is a feature nobody loves.</Line>
  <br />
  <Highlight>Great designers do both. That's the whole point.</Highlight>
  <Whisper>The world is full of people with good taste who never shipped. Don't be one of them.</Whisper>
</Principle>

<Principle num="09" title="Speed Comes From Ownership">
  <Line>Every handoff is a tax on your momentum.</Line>
  <Line>Every "waiting on dev" is a week you don't get back.</Line>
  <br />
  <Highlight>Ownership removes friction. Friction kills quality.</Highlight>
  <Whisper>The fastest designer on the team isn't the one who designs fastest. It's the one with the fewest handoffs.</Whisper>
</Principle>

<Principle num="10" title="Values Are Decisions">
  <Line>A color isn't a color until it ships to 29 brands.</Line>
  <Line>A spacing token isn't spacing until a real user scrolls past it.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>The JSON file is the design.</Line>
  <Line>The variable is the decision.</Line>
  <Highlight>Everything else is a sketch.</Highlight>
  <Whisper>When you edit a token in Git, you're not writing code. You're making a design decision that reaches millions of readers.</Whisper>
</Principle>

<Principle num="11" title="Shipping is the Only Proof">
  <Line>Not prototypes. Not decks. Not alignment meetings.</Line>
  <br />
  <Line>Did it ship?</Line>
  <Line>Did real people experience it?</Line>
  <Line>Did it make the product <Bold>better</Bold>?</Line>
  <Highlight>Production is the only truth.</Highlight>
</Principle>
